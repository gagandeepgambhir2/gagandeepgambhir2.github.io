A Trading Portal of Fashion Items

HauteTrader is for the fashionable girl, whose style is constantly evolving, yet feels she has nothing to wear. To her - my client offers a novel concept in online designer fashion trading! Members can simply trade the designer fashions that she has for the fashions that she craves. After every successful fashion trade, she gets a reward currency, Haute-Points, which she can use to buy more designer fashions . . ALL without breaking or even opening the bank!

Our Work

Created a very intuitive web application having all types of necessary business management tools for managing the whole process at different stages of their work. Development of this project from scratch to delivery where requirements understanding, designing the database structure, creating UML diagrams, development, and deployment were the various levels.

Challenges

Client wants to send the various instant emails like further steps from the current state of a member, trade reminders, shipping reminders, item listing statuses, etc.
