Introduction

"Ananas" is a comprehensive online training and learning platform, teaching hospitality staff the skills that employers hire for. Ananas has been developed for the industry which provides up-to-date information and material designed to help people to learn and succeed! Learning modules have been created in conjunction with some of the finest experts in React and Laravel. Detailed lessons feature infographics and informative videos, to make it easy and fun to learn. Detailed reporting tools to make informed decisions about the business.

Our Work

Managed general administrative duties like registering users and granting levels of access, and then there are instructor duties, which include creating and organizing courses.
Integrated third party content.
Developed a Rich-feature to analyze and interpret data to reproduce the reports accurately with charts and bars.
Implemented end-user support to provide training on how to use the more advanced features of Ananas.
