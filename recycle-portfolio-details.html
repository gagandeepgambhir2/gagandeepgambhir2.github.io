Introduction

"Recycle" , software tool provides a simple and reliable solution to scrap and recycle the metal. This is a complete end to end solution that helps the industry to monitors the product waste until it has been finally recycled by the recycling firm and keeps the record of disposed waste.

Our Work

Refining new requirements.
Adding new requirements identified as being required to achieve the software product vision statement.
Reviewing and setting the priorities assigned to the product backlog.
CMS with gargantuan features of administering workflow, content, and images.
Special attention has been paid to the real-time performance.

Features

The system collects and tracks each transaction that is processed by the user with the ability to manage, invoice, analyze and report on the data captured.
Clients can view videos or photos of the materials being processed for recyclers.
Email integration and can send reports in PDF format from within the system without downloading to a respective user.
Can know your container availability and the precise location and duration of assigned containers.
The PWA style of the web application has flexible configurations.

Outcome

Cloud-based waste management solution that helps small to medium roll-off businesses track to scrap yards, metal recyclers, plastic recyclers, waste processors, etc.
